FNotepad

About


FNotepad is a java-written Notepad. It is availible in English, German, Italian and French at the moment.
The latest version is v1.4.1

Features

  • File operations
  • Look and feel changer
  • Tab settings
  • Change easy between all languages
  • Change pad and text color
  • Letter and word counter
  • Search for letters and words
  • Open terminal
